About mossJobs

Welcome to mossJobs! The idea behind this app is to create a way for all of us in GA to share job listings we find with one another. We know there will be a struggle straight out of bootcamp, and that in a sense we are all competing with one another, but what if you come across a job where maybe you don't meet the requirements, or perhaps it's not the coding language that you'd prefer to do? Why not share it with another classmate that you think would be interested? We all want one another to succeed so lets try and help each other out along the way!

How it Works

The functionality of the site is simple. Below there's a form where you can fill out any job listings you think may interest a fellow classmate or friend. Once the information is up, users can go in and edit it if some of the information may not be correct, or perhaps they want to add more information to share. You can also delete the listing if someone had found out the position had been filled.
